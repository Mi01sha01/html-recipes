Fluffy Morning Pancakes

Start your day with these soft and fluffy pancakes, made with simple ingredients and ready in just a few minutes. Perfect for breakfast or brunch!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (240ml) milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter (plus more for cooking)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional, for flavor)

Instructions

  1. Mix the dry ingredients.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Preheat the pan over medium heat.
  5. Cook the pancake until bubbles form.
  6. Flip and finish cooking the other side.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite toppings.
